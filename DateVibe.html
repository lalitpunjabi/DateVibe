Service Description

We are an agency offering personalized tours and activities led by local experts passionate about sharing their personality, knowledge, and culture. We focus on providing authentic experiences that allow clients to immerse themselves in the history, gastronomy, art, and entertainment of our city through the eyes and stories of those who know it best: its inhabitants.

Market Segment

International tourists, frequent travelers, business executives, and anyone interested in exploring the local culture in an authentic and meaningful way.

Value Proposition

Connecting our clients with unique and enriching experiences, guided by passionate local experts. We offer a personalized service that adapts each experience to the specific needs and interests of our clients, ensuring that every encounter is memorable and deeply meaningful.

Services Offered

  • Themed Tours: Local history, architecture, urban art, etc.
  • Gastronomic Experiences: Culinary tours, tastings at local markets, cooking classes.
  • Artistic Activities: Art workshops, local music, theater, or dance.
  • Entertainment Events: Visits to local events, festivals, and shows.
  • Visits to Local Communities: Meetings with artisans, community projects, and local traditions.

Distribution Channels

  • Online Platform: Website for bookings, detailed descriptions of each experience, and client testimonials.
  • Local Alliances: Collaboration with hotels, restaurants, travel agencies, and local entertainment businesses.
  • Digital Marketing: Use of social media, content marketing, and SEO to promote our experiences and reach our target audience.

Revenue Model

Revenue from each experience sold, with variable pricing based on duration, personalization, and additional services requested by the clients. Fixed rate options and custom packages based on the client’s preferences.

Growth Strategy

  • Expansion of Offerings: Introduction of new tours and activities based on client feedback and local trends.
  • Internationalization: Exploration of international markets appealing to travelers interested in authentic cultural experiences.
  • Strategic Alliances: Collaborations with local influencers, event organizers, and brands to increase visibility and reach for our business.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To connect our clients with authentic and enriching experiences through local experts passionate about sharing their personality, knowledge, and culture.

Vision: To be recognized as leaders in creating unique cultural experiences that inspire, educate, and connect people with local communities around the world.
